The space
THE BLACK CABIN

The Black Cabin is a 3-storey A-frame cabin with 3.5 bedrooms with a full toilet and
bath in each bedroom and also comes with a powder room. The living room boasts of the
high-ceiling wooden arc of the A-frame house which has a great view of the mountains of Itogon.
The style and finishing of the house is more classic with focus on black and white hues in both
interior and exterior of the house. It is also more senior-friendly since the ground floor
as one bedroom where the dining and kitchen are located.

Living Room
Our living room boasts of a high-wooden ceiling with a 55" HD TV, bluetooth music player with an outdoor
balcony that provides mountain views.

Kitchen
We have a complete kitchen setup with refrigerator, electric cooktop, rice cooker, toaster and coffee maker.

Outdoor Veranda
We have outdoor chairs to hangout and griller for cook-outs

Guest access
The guests will have access to the entire house

Other things to note
There is plenty of parking in the house: 2 vehicles inside and 2 more outside
We have a charcoal grill for outdoor cooking. There is a room at every floor therefore,
senior-friendly since there is a room at the ground floor where the dining/ kitchen area is situated.
We allow small/ medium sized pets but they are only allowed to stay on the ground floor.
