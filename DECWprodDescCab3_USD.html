Casa de Buena

The space
Casa de Buena is a modern house that has an exquisitely-designed interiors that features an indoor swimming pool.

There are three spacious rooms and an entertainment area where you can enjoy videoke and other fun activities with friends and family.
A free wifi connection is included all throughout your stay.

Casa de Buena can comfortably accommodate 15 guests and highly recommended for families and barkada get-aways over a long weekend or holidays.

Guest access
Casa de Buena is located in Barangay Bigaa, Cabuyao City, Laguna and is 5 minutes away from the public market and other food establishments.
It is 45 minutes from Metro Manila and very accessible from Laguna's many Science Parks and Economic zones.
